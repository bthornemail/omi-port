Canonical URI-CIDR scope examples for omi-port v0.

Valid:
  file:///repo/input.omi#/64
  lora://915/node/alpha#/16
  https://example.com/api#/40

Invalid:
  https://example.com/api           (no #/ prefix)
  https://example.com#/abc          (non-numeric prefix)
  <300-char-uri>#/32               (URI exceeds OMI_PORT_URI_MAX)
